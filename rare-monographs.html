MARC Field Usage








Immediate Source of Acquisition Note - use to record donor or gift information.
5411/$a Gift of FirstName LastName, month year (if known).






Ownership and Custodial History - use to record provenance, when item was removed from manuscript collection. Include URL to manuscript collection record in Annie (eventually ArchivesSpace). The URL in $u will make a hyperlink of the text in $a.
5611/$a From the _______ Collection located in Leyburn Library Special Collections, Coll. #. $u http://....






Copy and Version Identification Note - use to record information that distinguishes one copy from another, often signatures or annotations. "Signature" should be used for a signature, "inscription" for a signature + note or other personalization.
562//$a [Whichever] page/copy has signature : FirstName LastName.
